Catherine P. Dilan

As I navigated the corridors of education, my academic pursuit led me to the path of enlightenment in Bachelor of Secondary Education with a major in Social Studies. Embracing the wisdom of Horace, I hold dear the belief that "Vitiis nemo sine nascitur" – that no one is born without faults. Through the chapters of my life, I strive to learn, grow, and weave my narrative into the rich tapestry of human experience.
